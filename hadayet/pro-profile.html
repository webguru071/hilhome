FAQs

What should the customer know about your pricing (e.g., discounts, fees)?

If you want to know more about us, behavior consulting and training, please visit our website

Behavior Modification $1990.00

This 2 week program is designed to address minor behavior issues (i.e. jumping, mouthing and counter surfing, etc.) as well as basic obedience: recall sit/down heel place

It is NOT for dogs with aggression issues or extreme levels of anxiety or fear. Depending on the intensity of the dog's issues additional weeks may be required (at $750 per wk). This recommendation will be made based on the initial consultation or shortly after the beginning of training.

What is your typical process for working with a new customer?

Typically I prefer to start the process with an in home consultation to get a complete picture of the dogs environment and its interactions with its owners.

Once I have met the animal I work with the owner to develop a program to address the dog's issues in a way that will promote real and lasting change.
