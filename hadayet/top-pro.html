FAQs

How do you measure how fast I respond?

We look at the amount of time it takes you to respond to a lead. If you reply to 3 out of every 4 leads within 4 business hours of them contacting you, you’ll be eligible for Top Pro. “Business hours” means between 8AM - 8PM local time — so if you get a message at 7PM, you have until 11AM the next morning to respond. And don’t worry, we also don’t count messages that you’ve been refunded for.
