Leads

What is a lead

A lead is a new customer who has seen what you offer and wants to learn more about working with you. You only pay the first time a customer messages you, requests a call or estimate, or asks for your availability.

Types of leads

Exact match

When a customer reaches out to you about a job that perfectly matches your targeting preferences. You pay for these leads automatically at a 20% discount, and you’re covered by our Price Assurance. Keep an eye on the "Direct leads" section of your Leads tab for exact matches.

Partial match

When a customer reaches out to you about a job that perfectly matches your targeting preferences. You pay for these leads automatically at a 20% discount, and you’re covered by our Price Assurance. Keep an eye on the "Direct leads" section of your Leads tab for exact matches.

Other opportunities

If you want more work, go to your Leads tab and click Opportunities. These customers already reached out to other pros. You can still let the customer know you’re interested in case the other pros can’t do the job. Keep in mind, if that customer responds to you, you pay the full price shown on the opportunity.
